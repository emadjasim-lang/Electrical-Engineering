Dr Emad Al-Mahdawi

Senior Lecturer in the Electrical & Mechanical Department

Biography

Emad Al-Mahdawi is a Senior Lecturer in the Electrical & Mechanical Department. He holds a PhD in Electrical Engineering and Systems and an MA with Distinction in Education Management and Leadership from the University of Chichester.

He is a Chartered Engineer with the IET and IMechE, and a Senior Fellow of the Higher Education Academy. He teaches and supervises MSc students at the Royal School of Military Engineering.

His research interests include electrical power system stability, renewable energy engineering, and innovative teaching and learning methodologies. He has contributed to the academic community through publications in internationally recognised Scopus-indexed conferences and journals.
